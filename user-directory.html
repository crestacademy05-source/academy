Why passwords aren't shown: CREST Academy stores each password as a salted SHA-256 hash, not as plain text. That's a one-way transformation — it can't be reversed to reveal the original password, by you or anyone else. This protects your students even if this data were ever seen by the wrong person. The "Password" column simply confirms a credential is on file. If a student is locked out, use your existing admin reset-password flow to issue them a new one.
